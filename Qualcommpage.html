2019 Summer Design & Research Intern

I had a huge opportunity to work as a design and research intern, focusing on augmented reality, for the summer of 2019 at Qualcomm on the Corporate User Experience team. Working with the senior director of the team, I truly learned a lot and had the chance to jump right into planning and executing a user research study on augmented reality devices, their video quality, and the feasibility for use by the everyday consumer. During my 3-month internship I learned so much and was truly blessed to be given the opportunity to work with a great team of designers and researchers.
