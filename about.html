Me



While pursuing my bachelor's in Computer Information Systems at Hampton University, I became interested in new technology and how it can be used to solve business problems. Users are very influential in whether or not a business will be successful and I realized that I wanted to be involved in the process of providing those users with the products and experiences they need and want. I care deeply about human-centered design and building world-class solutions and having a true desire to be in touch with users led me to HCI.

I am a first year student at the University of Maryland, College Park pursuing a master's in Human Computer Interaction. I currently live in the D.C. Metro area with a Pomeranian named Cinnamon and love to travel, play the violin, and golf.
